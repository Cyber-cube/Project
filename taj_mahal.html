TAJ MAHAL

Taj Mahal is the ultimate realisation of a great ruler. Emperor Shahjahan's dream for his beloved queen. One of the wonders of world, from 1631 A.D., it took 22 years in its making.

An estimated 20,000 persons worked to complete the delightful tomb, on the banks of Yamuna. For a breathtaking view of the Taj Mahal, one has to see it by moonlight.
